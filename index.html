Our Purpose

Mission Statement

"Home ownership is the cornerstone of a strong community."

If you have a place you can call "Home", you will have the will and courage to venture out and face any challenges life throws at you. As you face the challenges, you will have the peace of mind that at the end of the day, you will be relaxing in the comfort of your own home.

There are many in society who have not been as fortunate. Even though they have a piece of land, they don't have the resources or time to build a home on their property.

Swadesh Homes Foundation is committed to helping the impoverished in India realize their dream of home ownership.

Our goal is to identify and target specifically those individuals and families who have a piece of land but have not been able to build a home on their property. Swadesh Homes will help them by building a house which provides the basic comforts that all human beings deserve.

Swadesh Homes Foundation Inc is a New Jersey, USA-based public charity, exempt from Federal Income Tax under IRS Section 501(c)(3). We were formed by founding members with roots in Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh, India.

Our goal is to assist the economically weaker section of the population by providing assistance that fulfills their dream of owning a properly designed home — featuring a bedroom, kitchen, toilet, bathroom, running water, and electricity.

We have been partly inspired by the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY), the Central Government of India's scheme to assist the economically weaker sections with home building. Our work directly furthers the goals of PMAY.

Beneficiary Eligibility Criteria

  • ✓Family must own or legally inherit the land on which the home will be built
  • ✓Combined annual household income less than ₹2 lakhs
  • ✓All adult household members must possess Aadhaar cards
  • ✓Property must be co-owned by a female member of the family
  • ✓Property location must fall under Varanasi district, Uttar Pradesh

Our Story

Founded by a group of NRIs with deep roots in Varanasi, Swadesh Homes was born from a simple but powerful idea: we can use our position abroad to give back to the communities that shaped us.

Starting with just 6 families in Phase 1 (2022), we've now completed 6 phases and 29 homes — each one a story of dignity restored and futures secured.

What We Do

Our Services

Our services include but are not limited to planning, financing, and construction of homes — end-to-end.

Survey & Planning

We conduct a thorough land survey ensuring conformance with zoning restrictions and local laws before construction begins.

Design

Custom architectural designs to maximize utility, durability, and safety of every square foot — while keeping costs minimal.

Construction

Quality materials ensuring durability and minimal maintenance. Safety, utility, and comfort are the guiding principles.

Financing

We use donor funds to fully finance construction costs, ensuring families receive their homes without any financial burden.
