Mission Statement

If you have a place you can call “Home”, you will have the will and courage to venture out and face any challenges life throws at you. As you face the challenges, you will have peace of mind that at the end of the day, you will be relaxing at the comfort of your home…


There are many in society who have not been as fortunate. Even though they have a piece of land, they don’t have the resources or time to build a home on their property


Our initiative is to identify and target specifically those individuals and families that have a piece of land but have not been able to build a home on their property, and help them get a house built which they can call “Home”


For the first phase, we will start small by helping a small group of people who we directly know. Depending on the success level of the first phase, we will expand the initiative to others in subsequent phases

services

Our services include but not be limited to, planning, financing and construction of homes.

Survey and planning

We will conduct a survey of the land to make sure it is in conformance with any zoning restrictions and in accordance with the local laws...

Design

A proper design to maximize the utility of the available land is essential. We will use our own architects to do a custom design of the house to maximize utility, durability and safety, and minimize the cost of construction

Construction

We will use quality material to ensure durability and minimal wear and tear, thus reducing the need for maintenance. Safety, utility and comfort will be our goal
